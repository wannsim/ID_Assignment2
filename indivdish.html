Chicken Soup

About the dish: 

          Chinese-style fried rice is a dish that is widely consumed in China 
          and around the world.It is believed to have originated in the 
          southern Chinese city of Guangzhou (Canton) in the late 19th century,
           where it was created as a way to use up leftover rice from the previous 
          day. The dish soon became popular among Chinese immigrants in Southeast
          Asia, and from there,it spread to the rest of the world. 
          The basic ingredients for Chinese fried rice include rice, soy sauce, 
          egg, scallions, a protein such as chicken, beef, or shrimp. Over time,
          different regional variations of the dish have emerged, incorporating
          local ingredients and flavours. Today, Chinese-style fried rice is
          considered a staple dish in many households and is widely available at 
          Chinese restaurants around the world.

Ingredients: 

        Leftover rice 
        1  spring onion stalk 
        1 diced carrot 
        Corn 
        Peas 
        Egg 
        1 small onion 
        Protein of choice (Chicken/beef/pork) 

        Meat marinade 
        Ginger 
        Garlic 
        Soy sauce 
        Sugar 
        Sesame oil

steps:

Frying the eggs Scramble the beaten eggs into the pan and turn off 
the heat when egg is close to being done but not completely, set eggs 
aside 

Frying the chicken and vegetables 
Fry chicken in pan and remove when it turns golden brown, without 
washing the pan, put in the onions, carrots, and peas and stir fry until 
almost cooked through.  

After vegetables are slightly browned, add in the garlic and cook for 
another minute 

Putting it all together 
Add in the rice and stir fry until well mixed, at this point, 
add back in the eggs and chicken and season the rice with soy sauce and  

Plating 
Serve with sesame oil and green onions
